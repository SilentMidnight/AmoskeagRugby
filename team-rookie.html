Rookie Rugby

RRookie Rugby is the introductory game for rugby. It is a non-contact version of rugby developed for youth. Boys and girls ages 5-12 are welcome. Rookie Rugby is played at Thibeault Field in Manchester. To register or for more information, please email AmoskeagRookieRugby@gmail.com.
